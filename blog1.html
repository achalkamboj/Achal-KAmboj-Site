TOP 5 PUNJABI FOODSTHAT YOU SHOULD DEFINITELY TRY

1 August, 2021

Craving for some delicious treats? Wanna get some calories in? do not worry, we have a list of top 5 lip- smacking Punjabi foods that you should definitely try. From tangy to spicy, traditional to modern, nutritious to junk, we got you covered. The food of Punjab has its own charm. From festivals to prayers, everything revolves around food for Punjabis. The heavenly taste of these amazing Punjabi dishes will hands-down make you want more and the pictures of these top 5 Punjabi foods will definitely make you drool.

1. AMRITSARI KULCHA.

Amritsari Kulche-Chole is the staple Punjabi food when it comes to morning breakfast. Stuffed with potatoes, cauliflower, paneer and a variety of spices, the first bite of Amritsari kulcha will rush dopamine down your spine. Cooked in traditional Indian tandoor and served with loads of butter or ghee, spicy chole and chopped onions, no one and nothing will be able to stop you from overeating. This will be the juiciest and the most flavourful dish you will ever taste.

2. LASSI.

If you’re trying to grab typical Punjabi beverage anytime of the day, then a glass of chilled lassi is a must. Made with buttermilk, khoya, nuts and lots of love, this refreshing lassi will make you forget everything around. The smooth texture of this buttery lassi will make you wonder whether to scoop it or drink it. Try this cuisine and it will definitely become your daily craving buster.

3. MAKKI DI ROTI TE SARSON DA SAAG.

Who hasn’t heard the name of this Punjabi blessing to the world? Only the sight of Makke Di Roti layered with butter and the fragrance of Sarson Da Saag will attract you towards itself like a magnet. Mainly consumed in winters, it is high in nutrition and keeps the body warm. Tasty as well as highly rich in nutrients, this evergreen Punjabi dish is an art in itself. Only and only tasting it will make you realize why it is so famous in India.

4. DAL MAKHNI.

Your Punjabi lunch and dinner will be incomplete if you are going to eat it without world’s tastiest dal, DAL MAKHNI. This velvety, creamy and buttery dal is topped with lots and lots of cream and yogurt. For the best experience, pair it with a butter naan or a laccha paratha and be ready to ruin your dieting schedule.

5. PHIRNI.

Last but not the least, you will not want to miss this white colored tempting desert which is made with ground rice, milk and lots of dry fruits. Who would say no to a chilled Phirni loaded with nuts and decorated with rose petals? This creamy delectable desert is enough to turn your boring regular meal into a dreamy treat.

These were the top Top-5 Punjabi foods you must give a try. All these beautiful dishes are so tasty and satisfying that after enjoying these you will not feel like eating anything else. From sweet to spicy, from sour to piquant, you will never be able to get enough of the culinary heritage of Punjab.
